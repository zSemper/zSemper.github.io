Fluid Tanks
- Does Block at x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z] have a tank - Drain [Amount] mB from Tank of Block at x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z] - Drain [Amount] mB of [Fluid] from Tank of Block at x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z] - Fill Tank with [Amount] mB of [Fluid] at Block x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z] - Get Fluid from Block at x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z] - Get Amount of Fluid in Tank at x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z] - Get Size of Tank at x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z] - Can [Fluid] be inserted into Block at x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z] - Can Item in [Hand] of [Entity] interact with Tank of Block at x:[X] y:[Y] z:[Z]

Can be used to check if a given block at a specific block position has a tank. If the block contains a tanks the output is true.

Params:
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block
Return:
- true, if the block contains a tank
- false, if the block does not contain a tank

Used to drain any fluid out of the tank of a block at a specific block position.

Params:
- Amount: the amount that will be drained from the tank
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block

Used to drain a specified fluid out of the tank of a block at a specific block position.

Params:
- Amount: the amount that will be drained from the tank
- Fluid: the specific fluid in the tank that will be drained
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block

Used to fill a tank with a specific fluid and amount.

Params:
- Amount: the amount that the tank will be filled with
- Fluid: the fluid the tank will be filled with
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block

Used to get the specific fluid inside a tank of a block at a specific block position.

Params:
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block
Return:
- the fluid inside the block as a FluidStack

Used to get the amount of fluid inside a tank of a block at a specific block position

Params:
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block
Return:
- the amount in the tank

Used to get the size of a tank of a block at a specific block position

Params:
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block
Return:
- the maximum size of the tank

Can be used to check if a fluid can be put into a tank of a block at a speicific block position. This is only really applicable for blocks that have a fluid filter for the tank.

Params:
- Fluid: the fluid thats checked, if it can be put into the tank
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block
Return:
- true, if the fluid can be put into the tank (will return true for almost every tank)
- false, if the fluid cannot be put into the tank

Uses the item in the main or off hand to drain or fill the tank of a block at a specific block position and

Params:
- Hand: the hand used by the entity, can either be "MAIN_HAND" or "OFF_HAND"
- Entity: the player that right clicks on the block
- X: the x coord of the block
- Y: the y coord of the block
- Z: the z coord of the block
Return:
- true, if the action of filling the block from the item or draining the block with the item is successful
- false, if both actions fail
