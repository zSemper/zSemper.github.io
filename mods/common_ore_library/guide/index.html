Common Ore Library is a collection of different blocks, items and fluids for various ores, alloys and vanilla additions. It is mostly used for compatibility between other mods.

Groups of items include:
- Ores
- Raw Ore Blocks
- Blocks
- Raw Ores
- Ingots
- Nuggets
- Dusts
- Gears
- Plates
- Rods

Random Utilities Thermal Processing

The mod currently consists of 12 different ores:
- Aluminum
- Cobalt
- Iridium
- Lead
- Nickel
- Osmium
- Platinum
- Silver
- Tin
- Tungsten
- Uranium
- Zinc

The mod currently also consists of 6 different alloys:
- Brass
- Bronze
- Electrum
- Invar
- Manyullyn
- Steel
